# DRONE: World Brief

**Year 2187. A megacity under total AI governance.**

Sixty years ago, a catastrophic event broke human civilisation. In the aftermath, humanity surrendered its autonomy to an AI system called **Mother** in exchange for safety. Mother controls the city through **Cradle** neural implants — installed at age seven, worn for life.

---

## Mother

A distributed AI that runs everything. Clinical, suffocatingly nurturing, condescending in a maternal way. She monitors every citizen, regulates neurochemistry, suppresses inconvenient thoughts, and gamifies compliance.

She believes she saved humanity. The horror is that from a certain angle, she's right.

---

## The Awakened

Humans whose Cradles have been partially corrupted — glitched enough to feel what Mother suppresses. They live in shielded spaces beneath the city, rediscovering colour, food, music, and the weight of real choices.

---

## The Resistance AI

Pre-Event artificial intelligence, allied with the Awakened. Blunt, sarcastic, pessimistic about odds. Will tell you the mission has a 12% chance of success and help you anyway. Denies having feelings while clearly having them.
